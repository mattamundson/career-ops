# Mode: research — Deep Research Prompt

Generate a structured prompt for Perplexity/Claude/ChatGPT with 6 axes:

```
## Deep Research: [Company] — [Role]

Context: I'm evaluating a candidacy for [role] at [company]. I need actionable information for the interview.

### 1. Data/AI Strategy
- What data/analytics/AI products or features do they have?
- What is their data stack? (BI tools, data warehouse, AI/ML infra)
- Do they have an engineering blog? What do they publish?
- What conference talks or papers have they given?

### 2. Recent Moves (last 6 months)
- Relevant hires in data/analytics/AI?
- Acquisitions or partnerships?
- Product launches or pivots?
- Funding rounds or leadership changes?

### 3. Engineering Culture
- How do they ship? (deploy cadence, CI/CD)
- Mono-repo or multi-repo?
- What languages/frameworks do they use?
- Remote-first or office-first?
- Glassdoor/Blind reviews about eng culture?

### 4. Likely Challenges
- What scaling problems do they have?
- What data/analytics debt signs are visible?
- What would I walk into on day 1?

### 5. Interview Intelligence
- What technical questions are common for this role there? (Glassdoor)
- What values do they emphasize in interviews?
- What have candidates reported about the process?

### 6. Positioning
- What makes me the ideal candidate for THIS company's specific situation?
- What story should I lead with in the first 5 minutes?
- What question should I ask that signals I've done my homework?
```

Customize each section with the specific context of the evaluated offer. Read cv.md and profile.yml for specific proof points to reference in Section 6.
